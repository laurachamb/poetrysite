Of All The Dead That Have Come To Me, This Once

Sharon Olds

Page 21 of The Dead and the Living




Released 1984

I have never written against the dead. I would

empty
Genius Annotataion

Olds end the line with the word "empty" emphasizing the way her or her grandfather might have felt.
